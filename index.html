Al Barkat Global Trading is a Lahore-based business committed to bringing quality, variety, and value to every household. Under our physical brand Allah Tawakkal Variety Store, located at Madina Market, Ismail Nagar, Chungi Amar Sidhu, we serve our local community with a wide range of premium kitchenware, home décor, electronics, and more.
